# House of Heroes

## Overview
A thick-walled fortification dedicated to the god of war Tempus, the House of Heroes exalts and celebrates those who triumph above all, whether that is on the field of battle or in sporting competitions. Those who throw their bodies into the fray in search of victory and glory find a grand welcome here. A significant part of the temple is dedicated to the Academy of Arms, one of Waterdeep's premiere schools of the fighting arts. The thick outer walls of the temple house facilities familiar to those with a history of military service: barracks, mess halls, armories, all arrayed around a courtyard cobbled with stone for training and mustering. Rising from the middle of the courtyard is the sacred temple sanctuary called Foehammer Hall, where the rites of Tempus are performed.

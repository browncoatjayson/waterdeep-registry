# Nanny's Teatime

## Overview
_Tea House._ A beautifully appointed tea house that offers a quiet and reflective atmosphere along with a splendid array of domestic and exotic teas and snacks, owned and run by Nanny Rumtingle. Those who are in the know of such things are aware, however, that she has another business, secreted away in the basements: a mordayn vapor den! (A three story (with basement) ground-floor shop with apartments above (C) that is well-worn with heavy daily use)

### Cellars
**Basement Stall-Shops:** _Tiny Sales Stalls._ Nanny Rumtingle has subdivided the cellars beneath her teashop into a trio of narrow selling spaces with tiny storage backrooms. Each of these miniature shops (they are generally large enough for a single wall of sales goods, with room for shoppers to browse) tends to see a frequent turn-over of small businesses, mostly starters who are just beginning their careers…or who are on the verge of ending them. These three small spaces share a foyer at the bottom of the alley steps between the teahouse and Archibald's, with a small jakes off to one side.
**Secret Mordayn Den:** _Illicit Drug Den._ The sweet old Nanny has an abiding secret: she runs a subterranean drug den where she sells mordayn, or "dream-tea" to those customers who are in the know. Such folk excuse themselves to use one of her tea room jakes with a secret passage into a rickety stairwell that leads down into the den's lobby. There, one of Nanny's den "hosts" gets the visitor set up with their preferred dosage and a comfortable space in on of the dimly-lit dream dens, leaving them to enjoy their dreams in peace. Customers do not talk to one another here; indeed, they hardly tend to acknowledge one another's presence, by general convention.
### Second Floor
**Light in the Window:** _Chandlery._ The dwarven Cavebright sisters run this small candle shoppe, offering a wide variety of colored and scented candles in addition to a selection of basic household beeswax pillars, tapers, and other candles of every useful sort. One of the Cavebrights (Kirzah or Waldmara) are here during business hours while the other tends to shopping or making new candles in their small workshop.
**Pilinthar's Gentle Goods:** _Curios & Decor._ Lyranna Pilinthar runs the larger of the two shops, which is filled with décor and knickknacks of a bewildering and ever-changing catalogue. She has a great many contacts among the various guilds, having been raised by parents who were Guildsfolk, but is not a member of any herself. She does pick up the odds and ends that she sells in her shop for exceptionally good deals, turning around to display and promote them as "fine décor" in her shop, selling them for many times what she paid for them. She has made a fine living for herself in this way, and she seems content to let her business prosper out of this small shop and the rooms behind it.
**Suites:** The corridors behind the shops of Nanny's building connect the shops, the staircase at the back of the building, and a pair of two-room suites. The larger of the two is rented by Lyranna Pilinthar, divided into a bedroom and a sitting room, while the other suite is rented by the Cavebright sisters, providing a tiny workshop space for candlemaking and a living area for their daily lives.
### Third Floor
**Eternal Songs:** _Pet Shop._ The usually-open door and small cages of songbirds enjoying the air on the shopway ensures that Eternal Songs lives up to its name: the sounds of myriad songbirds, all trilling their various songs, can be heard all day, every day from here. Liagua Ildennar is the middle-aged human proprietress of this pet shop, and she seems to delight in the birdsong, often singing along with them in a terrible, off-key whistling of her own. She drifts through her shop like some sprightly fairy tale princess, talking to her birds and seeing to their needs, sparing just enough attention to her customers to ensure the (nominal) prosperity of her business. She largely ignores the boorish Laudeman next door, even when he comes ranting at her doorstep.
**Laudeman's Lutes:** _Luthier._ A guild member and graduate of New Ollamh, Master Effarin Laudeman sells lutes out of this shop (although he makes them at his own workshop in his home in the Castle Ward). He also buys old instruments and refurbishes them, although that's largely an excuse to examine the craftsmanship of other (often older) craftsfolk. Afflicted with perfect pitch, Laudeman is driven near to madness by the incessant racket from his next-door neighbor. Though Laudeman doesn't use it himself, he does maintain a workshop for his two apprentices Gauld Autumnseer (a spry and shy young half-elven fellow with distinctive orange-gold pupils) and Mirianda Tantellanthos (a Sembian beauty who traveled far to apprentice with the Council of Musicians, Instrument-Makers, and Choristers).
**Suites:** The small residences at the back of the shops are fully tenanted. The northernmost is rented by one Kirian the Sunny, a turncoin fest-lad who sleeps all day and parties all night, frequently returning to his rooms with a patron for the evening. Though Kirian is friendly, he is also distant (and unfortunately, one of Laudeman's apprentices Gauld has fallen head-over-heels for the young hustler). The other is rented by Liagua Ildennar, although she spends most of her time in her shop. Finally, the southernmost suite is rented by Master Laudeman for the occupation of his apprentices, giving them a shared sitting room and each a private bedroom. In exchange for such laudable accommodations, they remain nearby the shop after hours to ensure it isn't burglarized.

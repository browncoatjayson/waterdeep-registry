## Overview
_Seamstress._ A clothier shop owned by Zirisstra and Fezelvinn, a pair of drow sisters who have gained a reputation for fine garments that cater to swordswomen and lady-venturers. They even sell some garments with an enchantment that allows the clothing to repair itself, though the price for such is of course high.

# Emerald Sky Warehouse East

## Overview
The two warehouses that flank Summerleaf Tobacconist are both owned by the up-and-coming Emerald Sky Coster, a tiny consortium of about six independent caravan-masters, representing nearly two dozen small trading caravans. Like any coster, the Emerald Sky was founded for one purpose: to provide mutual assistance to all members of the group, for the benefit of everyone. They negotiate for goods and services as a coster, ensuring that they get good prices, and their star is surely rising. 

These warehouses currently serve as the coster's main place of business, although they do have a "storage warehouse" in the Dock Ward. Space in Waterdeep is of course at a premium, and some of the coster's leadership have been eyeing the tiny little shop between the warehouses. If it could be bought out and torn down, they might dramatically increase their available warehouse flooring, as well as add some decent business offices. Unfortunately, the Summerleaf family seems disinclined to sell…

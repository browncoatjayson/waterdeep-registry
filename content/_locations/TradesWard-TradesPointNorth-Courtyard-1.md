# Amphail's Finest

## Overview
A stables owned by House Ilzimmer, Amphail's Finest does sell some horses, notably the fine Amphail grey. For the most part, however, they simply rent out stable space and animal care to those folk in the city who own horses and need a place to stable them. It is a common sight in the courtyard to find a few of the grooms walking or riding horses to exercise them.

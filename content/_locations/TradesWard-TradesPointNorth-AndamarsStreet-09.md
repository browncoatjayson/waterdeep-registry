# Andamar Estates North

## Overview
"The Estates" are a trio of large buildings, with very basic living spaces for those who struggle to make ends meet. Unfortunately, they have something of a reputation as a den for thieves, drug-users, and other shady sorts. Though they are three separate buildings, some of the residents have constructed temporary bridges between windows, providing ins and outs of all sorts.

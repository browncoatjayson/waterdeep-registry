# The Monastery of the Sun

## Overview
The Monastery of the Sun, sometimes called in old records the Monastery of the Sun Soul, is a tranquil oasis in the often-chaotic Trades Ward North neighborhood. Surrounded on all sides by the many shops, homes, warehouses, and other structures central to life in Waterdeep, the Monastery of the Sun is a graceful building notable for the great glass dome surmounting the round garden-sanctuary at one end, and the dual chapel of Lathander and Aumanator – an absolute work of art in its use of light – on the other.

The people of the neighborhood have a respectfully distant relationship with the sun soul monks here. While the monks welcome their neighbors – in particular, they encourage locals to take walks through their gardens, sit and join the monks at their meditations, or even arrange their own rites of passage within the monastery's chapel – the locals all know that the monastery is intended as a retreat of sorts. It's not a temple, after all; the ascetics here seek peace and contemplation, and by the gods their neighbors are intent on giving it to them.

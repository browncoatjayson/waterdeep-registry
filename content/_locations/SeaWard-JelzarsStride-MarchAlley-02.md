# Lavah's Triumphal Arms

## Overview
_Small Arms & Armor (C,2)._ Zhomish Lavah is the third son of a Turmian mercenary family who fell in love with a dashing priestess of Tempus. He followed her home to Waterdeep after they married, and although she passed into Tempus' fields a while ago, he continues to run the business they established. Everyone knows the Wares are an excellent source for fine arms and armor, generally tending toward the lighter side of things, although Zhomish certainly has connections enough to secure heavier gear for those who come seeking it. Zhomish's eldest son is likewise a spellcasting priest of Tempus, providing his father's business with minor enchantments on arms and armor for those seeking such.

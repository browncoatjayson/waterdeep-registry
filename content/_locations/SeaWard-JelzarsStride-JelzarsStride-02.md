# The Gladiator's Blade

## Overview
A modest tavern run by a retired gladiator famous for her bouts on the Field of Triumph, the Blade caters primarily to those who compete at the Field, to members of the Guard, and to warriors of all sorts. It's a fair place to find blades for those who need to hire them, although the wise are warned that those who are recruited from here are likely to favor their personal glory over the orders of someone who has hired them...but sometimes that's exactly what an employer seeks.

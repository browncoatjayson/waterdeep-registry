# Old Summerleaf's Pipes

## Overview
_Tobacconist._ The Summerleaf halfling family is well-known throughout the Delimbyr Vale for the quality of their smokeleaf. Minda Summerleaf sells her family's goods here, and her husband Rudley hand-carves fine wooden pipes for their customers as well. Summerleafs have operated this shop for three generations, and they're a fixture in the neighborhood.

# Hin-Home

## Overview
Though a relatively small building, this rooming house is set up to accommodate hin (as halfling folk often refer to themselves). The tall first floor actually has a floor/ceiling installed at about the five-foot height, dividing its rooms into "upper" and "lower" rooms, effectively doubling the living space in the small home. The basement has not had similar construction, but is used for storage and for working spaces that may be used by residents. (A one story (with basement) rooming house (D) that is undergoing renovations due to age)
